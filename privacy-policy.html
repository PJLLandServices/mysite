01Introduction

Welcome to www.pjllandservices.com (the "Website"). At PJL Land Services, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the information you provide to us.

02Information We Collect

We may collect various types of information when you visit our Website, including:

We may use the information we collect for the following purposes:
