Commercial properties we work on.

PJL is founder-led, so the realistic project size is small-to-medium commercial — properties measured in zones, not acres. Below is the typical mix.

🏘️

Condo corporations

Townhouse and condo complex common areas. Property-manager-friendly reporting, written drawings on file for the board, smart controllers managed remotely.

⛪

Churches & religious properties

Lawn, garden beds, and memorial-area irrigation. Scheduled around services and events. Volunteer-board-friendly fixed-price quoting.

🏢

Professional buildings

Office buildings, medical / dental plazas, professional services. Curb appeal matters — clean turf and beds without the in-house maintenance burden.

🛒

Retail strips

Small retail plazas, strip-mall landscape strips, parking-island irrigation. Tight scheduling around tenant operating hours.

🕊️

Funeral homes & banquet halls

Properties where appearance matters every single day. Reliable, low-failure systems with redundancy where the visit cadence demands it.

🏬

Small commercial buildings

Independent businesses, light-industrial offices, mixed-use buildings. Custom-quoted to scale.

Honest scoping — what we don't do

PJL doesn't take on sports fields, golf courses, agricultural / farm irrigation, or large industrial sites. Those need specialized equipment and crew sizes outside our scope. If your property fits one of those categories, we'd rather refer you to someone better-suited than over-promise.

Commercial services we provide.

Active service season runs April through October across the GTA. Through the winter we're available for site consultations, project planning, and next-season quotes — but live installs, seasonal openings, and most repairs happen during the active season. Indoor shut-off valve emergencies are the rare winter exception. Pricing scales with property size — fixed-price written quote after a site visit, no estimating-by-phone.

Why property managers pick PJL.

The same things that work for residential customers translate cleanly to small-to-medium commercial — sometimes better, because property managers are even more sensitive to the things commercial irrigation contractors get wrong.

Direct contractor accountability

No franchise routing. No sales rep handing the project to a crew you've never met. Patrick Lalande oversees every project end-to-end — quote, design, install day, commissioning, walk-through, and warranty calls in year three. The person who quoted the work is the same point of contact you call when something goes wrong.

Written everything

Fixed-price written quotes after the site visit — no "starting at" pricing, no surprise upcharges on install day. Written design drawings the board can keep on file. Written commissioning report after install. Property managers love a paper trail; we work the way they do.

Smart controllers as standard

Every commercial install includes a Hunter Hydrawise smart controller. The property manager (or board) gets a remote dashboard with water-usage tracking, failure alerts, and the ability to pause zones during paving / landscaping work without a service call. Reduces emergency-call costs by ~30-40% over old time-clock systems in our experience.

Year-three warranty that we'll actually honour

3-year parts-and-labour warranty on every install — well above the industry-standard 1-year. PJL has been operating since 2015, and we're not going anywhere. The warranty matters because we'll still be here to honour it.
