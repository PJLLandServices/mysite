Vaughan irrigation — a city of distinct neighborhoods, each with different needs.

Vaughan isn't really one place. Maple has the new builds and tight subdivision lots. Kleinburg has the estates and acreage. Thornhill has the mature established neighborhoods with significant tree canopies. Woodbridge has the cultural mix of old village core plus newer expansion. Concord is a commercial-industrial belt that needs a completely different irrigation conversation. Each one needs a different approach — and we tailor every quote accordingly.

What stays the same across all of Vaughan is the heavy clay soil (same as Newmarket and Aurora — slow water absorption, needs cycle & soak programming) and the City of Vaughan watering-restriction bylaws that smart controllers like Hydrawise handle automatically.

Vaughan watering bylaws — your sprinkler should follow them automatically

Vaughan typically restricts lawn watering to alternate days based on house number (odd/even) during summer water-conservation periods. Old timer-based controllers don't know about this — they just keep running the schedule you set. Hydrawise, programmed properly, automatically respects municipal watering windows so you stay compliant without thinking about it. We set this up on every Vaughan install as standard.
