The PJL story

I started PJL Land Services in Newmarket in 2015. The plan was simple: install and service sprinkler systems the way I'd want one done at my own house — proper hydrozoning, smart controllers as standard not as an upsell, fixed pricing, and a 3-year warranty that actually means something.

Ten years and a few thousand service calls later, the plan hasn't really changed. We've grown from one truck to a small crew, added landscape lighting alongside irrigation, and gotten certified on every Hunter and Hydrawise platform that exists. But the same person who answers the phone at 7 a.m. for a panicked "my system is flooding the basement" call is the same person — me — who shows up at the door 90 minutes later with a wrench.

How we actually work

A few things that aren't standard in this industry that we treat as standard:

  • Fixed-price quotes, written, on the spot. No "starting at" pricing, no surprise upcharges on install day.
  • Free design consult. 45 minutes, no commitment, no high-pressure sales. If your system doesn't need work, we'll say so.
  • Hunter Hydrawise smart controller standard on every install. Not an add-on. The water-savings pay for the controller in 1-2 seasons.
  • 3-year full warranty on parts and labour. Industry standard is 1 year on parts only.
  • Same-day repair across our service area between April and October. If we can't reach you the same day, we tell you that on the phone.

What we believe

  • Honest diagnostics. If your system doesn't need replacement, we won't pretend it does. The repair vs replace conversation is always math, not pressure.
  • Quality parts, every time. Hunter or Rain Bird only. Box-store generics fail in 2-3 years and erase the cost savings ten times over.
  • Local accountability. We live in the GTA. We're not a franchise route. If we screw up, you'll see us at Sobeys.
  • Pricing transparency. Every blog post on this site lists real prices. We're confident enough in our value that we put them in writing.
