The Stouffville-specific challenge

Eastern Stouffville sits on the Oak Ridges Moraine — sandier, well-drained soils that demand different head selection, runtime profiles and pressure regulation than the heavier York Region clay further west. A sprinkler system designed on a templated soil assumption will under-water the moraine areas and over-water the clay sections. We measure on-site and design zones around what's actually under your turf.
