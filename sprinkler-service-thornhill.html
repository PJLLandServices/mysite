Thornhill needs designs that respect what's already there.

Thornhill is unusual in our service area because it isn't a single municipality — it spans both Vaughan (west of Yonge) and Markham (east of Yonge), and is one of the most architecturally established neighbourhoods in York Region. Heritage streetscapes in Old Thornhill, curated landscapes in Royal Orchard, upscale custom builds in Thornhill Woods, mature-tree mid-century streets in German Mills and Bayview Glen.

That means a templated install doesn't work here. Mature trees create shade zones that need different watering than open lawn. Heritage hardscape limits where we can trench. Established planting beds need drip lines that respect the existing root systems. Premium architecture demands lighting design that complements, not overwhelms.

PJL has installed and serviced systems across Thornhill since 2015 — Newmarket-based and founder-led, Hunter Hydrawise certified, fully licensed and insured. 3-year warranty on every install.

Why Thornhill matters for landscape lighting specifically

The combination of mature-tree canopy, established hardscape and premium architecture means Thornhill properties benefit more from thoughtful lighting than almost any other neighbourhood in our service area. Architectural uplighting on heritage facades. Path lighting through mature gardens. Downlighting from existing trees. The infrastructure is already there — good lighting design just shows it off properly.
