Where Newmarket sprinkler systems are at right now (2026)

Most Newmarket subdivisions built between 2005-2012 are now in the "everything is starting to fail at the same time" window. Original valves are reaching end of life, controllers from that era are pre-WiFi, and 1st-generation pop-up heads are crumbling under the snowblower. We see a lot of "should I repair or just replace the whole thing" calls. Honest answer: it's almost always a partial retrofit (new controller, new valves, refurb the heads) rather than a full tear-out. We'll do that math with you on a free site visit.
