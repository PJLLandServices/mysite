Bring your existing customer list into the CRM.

Drop a spreadsheet — we'll match column headers automatically and show you exactly what'll be created or updated before you commit. Re-running the import later only updates the fields that are still empty, so admin-curated edits never get clobbered.

1. Upload your customer spreadsheet

Supports .xlsx, .xls, .csv. Multi-sheet workbooks: pick the sheet on the next step. Your file is parsed entirely in your browser — nothing leaves your computer until you click "Confirm import."

2. Pick the sheet to import

Choose the sheet that has your customer rows. Other sheets (schedule, work orders, etc.) can be imported separately later.

3. Confirm the column mapping

We auto-detect common header names. Override any that look wrong. Skip a column by setting it to "—".
