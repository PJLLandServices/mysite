Material lists Purchase orders Suppliers Catalog

Catalog is the master list of every part PJL stocks or installs. Add new SKUs as you start using them, edit prices when suppliers change theirs, and assign each SKU to its primary supplier. Material lists and purchase orders pull from this catalog — unassigned SKUs block PO generation, so it’s worth keeping suppliers populated as the catalog grows. Existing accepted quotes are unaffected by price changes here; their prices are locked in at quote creation.
