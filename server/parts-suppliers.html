Catalog assignments map each SKU in your parts catalog to a supplier. When you generate a Purchase Order from a material list, PJL groups the outstanding need lines by their assigned supplier and emits one PO per vendor. Unassigned SKUs block PO generation, so it's worth populating this table once and updating as suppliers change.
