iPhone Calendar sync

Subscribe your iPhone Calendar app to your PJL schedule. Confirmed bookings appear automatically; the calendar refreshes itself roughly hourly. Read-only — edits on your phone don't write back to PJL.

Calendar URL

Paste this into iPhone Settings → Calendar → Accounts → Add Account → Other → Add Subscribed Calendar.

Setup instructions (iPhone)
  1. Generate the URL above and tap Copy URL.
  2. On your iPhone: Settings → Calendar → Accounts → Add Account → Other → Add Subscribed Calendar.
  3. Paste the URL. iOS verifies it and adds a calendar called PJL Land Services.
  4. Open the Calendar app — your bookings appear in the calendar list.

How often it refreshes: iPhone polls subscribed calendars roughly every hour by default — there's no way for PJL to push faster than Apple polls. The server itself rebuilds the feed within ~5 minutes of any change, so a deleted or cancelled booking drops out of the feed quickly, but the phone may show it stale for up to an hour. To force a refresh now: open the Calendar app → tap Calendars at the bottom → pull down the list. iOS re-fetches every subscribed calendar immediately.

Security note: the URL itself is the credential — anyone with the link sees your schedule. Don't share it. If it leaks, hit Regenerate; the old URL stops working immediately and you'll need to re-add the new one to your phone.

What's in the feed: confirmed bookings only (cancelled and deleted ones drop out automatically). 90 days of history + 1 year forward. Event title is "<service> - <street>, <city>"; location is the full address in the form iOS Maps auto-recognizes (single-line, comma-separated, … , ON L4A 1A1, Canada).

Tax & income mapping

Required before invoices or items can sync. PJL will use these for every line PJL pushes.

HST tax code

QB applies this to every invoice line we push. Pick the 13% Ontario HST code your QB company uses for sales.

Default income account

Used as the IncomeAccountRef for every QB Item we create during items sync.

Auto-push

Off by default — flip on once you've validated a couple of manual pushes.

Auto-push estimates when accepted

When a customer signs a formal quote, push it to QB as an Estimate within ~30 seconds.

Auto-push invoices on completion cascade

When the WO completion cascade drafts an invoice, push it to QB and email it.

Items sync

One QB Item per pricing.json key + parts.json SKU. Idempotent — re-running only updates items whose price has changed.
