What Hydrawise actually does.

Most generic sprinkler timers run a fixed schedule whether or not it's actually needed. Hydrawise is a smart WiFi controller that adjusts your watering based on real conditions — and lets you see (and control) everything from your phone.

🌧️

Real-time weather skipping

Pulls forecast data from local weather stations and skips runs when rain is coming. Throttles back on cool days, runs longer on hot ones. No more Tuesday-morning sprinklers running through a Monday-night thunderstorm.

📱

Full app control

Run a zone, suspend the schedule, change a runtime, check what watered when — all from your phone. Works whether you're in the backyard or on vacation in another country.

⚠️

Smart leak alerts

If a zone runs longer than expected, pressure drops mid-cycle, or the controller can't reach the WiFi, your phone gets a ping within an hour. A stuck-open valve doesn't run for a week — it runs until you see the alert and shut it off.

📊

Zone-by-zone history

Every run, every duration, every weather decision logged. Becomes invaluable for diagnostics later — "this zone has been running 2 minutes shorter for three weeks" surfaces a clogged head before you'd notice the brown patch.

Average York Region homeowner saves 30-40% on seasonal water bills after a Hydrawise retrofit. On a typical 4-6 zone residential lot, that's $80-$160 saved per season. The retrofit pays for itself over multiple seasons, then keeps saving for the lifetime of the system — and the alerts, app control, and runtime history are a permanent bonus on top.

Will Hydrawise work with my system?

Almost always yes — Hydrawise is compatible with most existing residential and light-commercial irrigation systems regardless of original brand.

We've successfully retrofitted Hydrawise onto:

  • Hunter Pro-C, X-Core, X2, ICC2, ACC controllers (these are the easiest — we're swapping Hunter for Hunter)
  • Rain Bird ESP-Me, ESP-LX, SST, ESP-RZX
  • Toro Evolution, TMC-212, Custom Command
  • Orbit B-hyve, Easy-Set, sprinklertimer.com generic
  • Irritrol Total Control, KwikDial, Junior series
  • Generic builder-grade — most "no-name" controllers installed by builders are wired to standard 24-volt valve standards and swap straight in

The few cases where retrofit gets complicated:

  • Commercial multi-controller installations — if your system runs 30+ zones across multiple controllers, we usually want to assess on-site before quoting.
  • Hardwired flow sensors or master valves — supported by Hydrawise but the wiring needs verification.
  • WiFi reach — your home WiFi has to reach the controller's mounting location. We test this at quote stage.

If you're not sure whether your system qualifies, send us a photo of your existing controller and the zone count. We'll confirm before booking.

Why a smart controller alone isn't enough.

A Hydrawise retrofit improves how your system thinks. But if your sprinkler heads are over-pressurized and misting, or your nozzles aren't matched to the heads, the smart controller is just managing a flawed system more efficiently.

⚙️

Tech-Grade Standard on every PJL install

Pressure-regulated heads + low-pressure nozzles on every new install. Most companies upcharge for this. We don't — because efficient water needs both a smart brain (Hydrawise) and proper hydraulics. Both, every time.

If you're considering a retrofit on a system that has known coverage issues, dry spots, or visible misting at the heads, we'll often recommend addressing those during the same visit — pressure-regulated head replacement is $68 each ($95 service call applies once).
