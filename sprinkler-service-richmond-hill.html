Richmond Hill irrigation done by people who understand the soil.

Richmond Hill is the most geographically diverse municipality we serve. The northern half (Oak Ridges, Jefferson, North Richvale) sits on the Oak Ridges Moraine — sandy, well-draining soils that need completely different head selection and runtime programming than the heavy York Region clay we deal with in Newmarket and Aurora. The southern and central neighborhoods (Mill Pond, Crosby, Harding, South Richvale) are mature established areas with significant tree canopies that standard new-build sprinkler designs simply don't account for.

Most one-size-fits-all irrigation contractors get this wrong. We don't. Every Richmond Hill quote is designed around the specific soil profile and tree coverage of your actual property — not a generic template.

Oak Ridges Moraine — what most installers miss

The moraine drains fast. Standard 30-minute rotor zones that work in clay-soil Aurora will leach water past the root zone in Oak Ridges, wasting it and starving the lawn. We program shorter, more frequent cycles in moraine zones, and lean on MP rotators (slow application rate) rather than standard sprays. The result is better lawns and lower water bills — but it requires designing around the geology, not against it.
