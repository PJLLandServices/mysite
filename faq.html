General

What areas do you serve?

Yes — PJL Land Services is fully licensed and insured. We're also a certified Hydrawise installer, trained directly by Hunter Industries. You can have complete confidence in the quality and safety of our work.

Do you work on commercial properties as well as residential?

+

Yes — we work on both residential and commercial properties throughout the GTA. Whether it's a small residential backyard or a large commercial landscape, we have the experience and equipment to get the job done right.

We'll always be honest with you. During a diagnosis visit, we assess the full system and give you a clear recommendation. Most systems can be repaired rather than replaced — we'll only suggest replacement when it's genuinely the most cost-effective long-term option for you.

Sprinkler Systems

What smart controller do you use?

+

All our systems are installed with Hydrawise WiFi smart controllers by Hunter Industries — the same technology used by professional irrigation companies worldwide. These automatically adjust watering schedules based on real-time weather data, saving water and keeping your lawn healthier with zero effort from you. This is included as standard in every installation, not an add-on.

How many zones do I need?

+

Each irrigation zone typically covers 2,000–2,500 sq ft of lawn area. Most residential properties require between 3–6 zones depending on the size, shape, and complexity of the landscape. We'll assess your property during a free consultation and recommend the exact number of zones needed for full, efficient coverage.

Will the installation damage my lawn?

+

Minimal disruption is always our goal. We use professional trenching equipment that creates a narrow, clean trench and we take care to restore the surface as close to its original condition as possible. Most lawns show very little sign of installation within a few weeks once the grass fills back in.

Can you upgrade my existing system to smart controls?

+

Yes — this is one of our most popular services. Hydrawise smart controllers are compatible with most existing irrigation systems, regardless of brand. We can retrofit your existing system with a Hydrawise controller, giving you remote access, weather-based scheduling, and flow monitoring without replacing the entire system.

Seasonal Services

Do I need to winterize my sprinkler system every year?

+

Absolutely — in Ontario's climate, a professional fall closing is essential. When temperatures drop below freezing, any water left in your irrigation pipes and heads can expand, crack pipes, and damage fittings. A proper blow-out with our commercial compressor removes all water from the system, protecting your investment through the winter.

When should I book my spring opening?

+

In the Newmarket and GTA area, the safe window for spring openings is typically mid-April through May, once overnight temperatures are consistently staying above freezing. We recommend booking in early spring — our schedule fills up quickly every year and we want to make sure you get your preferred date.

What's included in a spring opening?

+

Our spring opening includes: slowly pressurizing the system to check for leaks or winter damage, running each zone individually to inspect all heads, adjusting spray patterns and arc settings for full coverage, programming your Hydrawise controller for the current season, and identifying any repairs needed. We leave with everything running perfectly and your Hydrawise app set up for the season.

When should I book my fall closing?

+

We recommend booking your fall closing for October, before the first hard frost. Waiting too long risks freeze damage. As with spring openings, our fall schedule fills up quickly — book early to secure your spot.

Landscape Lighting

Do you offer design as well as installation?

+

Yes — we design and install complete custom lighting systems. Every project starts with a free design consultation where we walk your property, discuss your goals and budget, and create a custom lighting plan. You'll see the full design before we install a single fixture.

What type of fixtures do you use?

+

We use only high-quality LED fixtures specifically selected for durability in Ontario's climate — from harsh winters to hot, humid summers. LED fixtures use a fraction of the energy of older halogen systems, last significantly longer, and produce better light quality.

How long does a landscape lighting installation take?

+

Most residential landscape lighting installations are completed in a single day. Larger properties or more complex designs may take two days. We'll give you a clear timeline during your free consultation.
