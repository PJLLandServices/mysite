Estate-scale irrigation done by people who've seen it before.

King City is unlike anywhere else in our service area. The lots are bigger — 0.5 to 5+ acres is common. The plantings are more elaborate — formal gardens, specimen trees, hedge mazes, fruit orchards. Many properties run on well water, which changes everything about pressure design and pump selection. And the renovation cycle is faster — King City homeowners renovate landscapes more often than the average GTA property.

This is the work we love. PJL has installed and serviced dozens of estate properties across King City and the broader King Township since 2015. We know the difference between a 4-zone subdivision install and a 22-zone estate that needs hydrozoning across formal beds, lawn sweeps, mature canopy trees, perennial borders, and a vegetable garden — all on different schedules.

What estate irrigation actually requires

For 1+ acre properties we typically use a multi-controller setup (one Hydrawise unit per zone group), separate tree-irrigation zones (RZWS or HDL drip per the Hunter brochure standard), well pump pressure regulation if you're not on municipal water, and a documented zone map you keep on file forever. Most estate installs run $12,000-$40,000+ depending on scope, with 3-year full warranty across the whole system.
