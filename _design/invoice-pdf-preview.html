Field-by-field data bindings

Every visible field on the invoice maps to one of four sources: STATIC values that never change; CONFIG admin-editable settings; DYNAMIC fields populated from the invoice or customer record at render time; COMPUTED values calculated from line items. Nothing is hardcoded inside the PDF generator — invoice-pdf.js reads everything from data/invoices.json, data/settings.json, and a small block of company constants.

Header

Visible fieldSourceBacking fieldNotes
"Invoice" titleSTATIC"Invoice" stringSwitches to "Quote" / "Receipt" on those document variants.
Company name + addressSTATICCOMPANY constants in invoice-pdf.jsSingle source for header + footer; reused across quote-pdf and po-pdf.
Email · phone · websiteSTATICCOMPANY.{email, phone, website}
GST/HST registrationSTATICCOMPANY.hstNumberCRA-required on every invoice over $30.
PJL logoSTATICserver/assets/logo-dark.svgDark variant to be added to repo (build brief flags).

Bill-to contact lineDYNAMICinvoice.bill_to.{phone, email}Hidden if both blank.
Ship-to columnDYNAMICinvoice.ship_toIf ship_to is null OR matches bill_to, the column is omitted entirely (single-column layout).
