Custom-quote items.

Some work is too variable to flat-rate fairly. For these, we'll either quote on-site after a quick visit or based on photos and a description through the contact form.

  • New zone install — starting at $575. Variables: head count, trenching distance, soil conditions, existing system load.
  • Mainline / frozen supply line repair — material varies (copper or poly), excavation depth and access vary, hard to flat-rate.
  • Smart upgrades / accessories — rain sensors, Hydrawise flow meters, soil sensors, decoder modules. Quoted based on which products and how they integrate with your controller.
  • Drip irrigation zones — typical bed-conversion or vegetable-garden drip retrofit is quoted on-site after a free design walk-through. Drip irrigation details →
  • Sprinkler installation (full new system) — quoted on-site after a free design walk-through. Written quote within 3-5 business days. Installation details →
  • Landscape lighting — fully designed projects priced after a daylight site assessment plus an after-dark design walk-through. Lighting details →

What we don't service — backflow assemblies.

Backflow testing & certification — not offered

Ontario regulates backflow testing and certification, and the work requires a certified backflow tester. PJL does not hold that certification. If your backflow assembly needs testing, certification, or service work, we'll refer you to a certified tester in your area. Visual checks of the backflow assembly during spring openings and fall winterizations are included — but we won't open, test, or sign off on the device itself.

One important disclaimer: these are our standard locked rates and they cover the vast majority of work we do. Genuinely unusual situations (badly damaged systems, hard-to-reach valve boxes, multi-controller commercial sites, drought-stage emergency repair conditions) may need on-site assessment to quote properly. We'll always quote the variation upfront before starting work — never on the invoice.
