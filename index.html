FAQ

Common Questions

What areas do you serve?

+

We serve Newmarket, Aurora, Barrie, Richmond Hill, Bradford, Innisfil, Keswick, Georgina, East Gwillimbury and the surrounding GTA. Not sure if we cover your area? Give us a call at (905) 960-0181.

How much does a lawn sprinkler system cost?

+

Every system starts with a $585 base installation fee plus $525 per zone. Most residential properties require 3–6 zones. Use our free online estimator for a ballpark figure, or book a free on-site consultation for an exact quote.

Is there a warranty on your installations?

+

Yes — every system we install comes with a full 3-year parts and labour warranty. We stand behind our work completely.

Do I need to winterize my sprinkler system every year?

+

Yes — in Ontario's climate, a professional fall closing is essential to prevent freeze damage to your pipes and heads. We offer fall closing services throughout the GTA every season.
