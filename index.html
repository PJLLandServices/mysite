❓ FAQ The questions we hear every day

Common Questions

What areas do you serve?

+

Two tiers. Core 9 cities with dedicated service-area pages: Newmarket, Aurora, East Gwillimbury, King City, Richmond Hill, Whitchurch-Stouffville, Vaughan, Markham, Thornhill. Extended coverage across the broader GTA, Simcoe County, Durham Region and Peel: Bradford, Innisfil, Barrie, Keswick, Georgina, Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Caledon, Pickering, Whitby, Oshawa — anywhere within ~90 minutes of Newmarket. See our coverage map or call (905) 960-0181.

How much does a lawn sprinkler system cost?

+

Pricing follows our 3-tier system. Tier 1 (1-4 zones): $585 base + $549 per zone, instant-quoted via our calculator. Tier 2 (5-7 zones): $749 base + $549 per zone, instant-quoted. Tier 3 (8+ zones): site visit and drawings required, custom-quoted. Use our online estimator for a 2-minute quote, or book a free consultation for larger systems.

Is there a warranty on your installations?

+

Yes — every system we install comes with a full 3-year parts and labour warranty. We stand behind our work completely.

Do I need to winterize my sprinkler system every year?

+

Yes — in Ontario's climate, a professional fall closing is essential to prevent freeze damage to your pipes and heads. We offer fall closing services throughout the GTA every season.
