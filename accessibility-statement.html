Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to a website that works for everyone — and how to tell us when it doesn't.

Last reviewed: January 03, 2026

01Introduction

PJL Land Services maintains commitment to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities and applies relevant standards.

02Accessibility Standards

The organization designs its website considering the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG): Our website aims to conform to Level AA of the WCAG 2.1 guidelines.

03Accessibility Features

Implementation includes:

Alternative text

Descriptions on images so screen readers can convey them.

Keyboard navigation

Browse the entire site without a mouse.

Clear language

Plain wording throughout — no jargon for jargon's sake.

Resizable text

Text scales cleanly with your browser's zoom controls.

Skip to content

A keyboard shortcut to bypass the navigation.

04Ongoing Efforts

The company works continuously to improve accessibility. If you encounter a barrier on this site, or have feedback about accessibility, please contact us:

05Conformance Status

The site is partially conformant with WCAG 2.1 Level AA. Partially conformant means that some parts of the content do not fully conform to the accessibility standard.

06Feedback

We welcome accessibility feedback to drive improvements that benefit all users. Your reports help us prioritize fixes — please don't hesitate to get in touch.
