Aurora's local irrigation contractor — based 10 minutes north in Newmarket.

Aurora sits on the same heavy York Region clay soil as the rest of north GTA — which means every standard "rule of thumb" you'll find in a generic blog post about sprinkler watering doesn't quite work here. Clay holds water, but accepts it slowly. Standard spray nozzles dump water faster than the soil can absorb. Without proper hydrozoning, cycle & soak programming and the right head selection, a brand-new sprinkler system in Aurora can produce a worse-looking lawn than no system at all.

That's where we come in. PJL Land Services has been installing and maintaining residential and commercial irrigation across Aurora since 2015. We're a Newmarket-based small crew (not a franchise route), Hunter Hydrawise certified, fully licensed and insured. Same person who quotes the job is the one running the install.

The Aurora-specific challenge

Aurora's older neighborhoods (Aurora Heights, Town Park, Regency Acres) sit on mature trees and significant elevation changes. The newer subdivisions (Bayview Wellington, Aurora Grove, Bayview Northeast) have flatter lots but tighter property lines and HOA hardscaping rules. Different neighborhoods need different irrigation strategies — we tailor every quote to the property, never to a template.
